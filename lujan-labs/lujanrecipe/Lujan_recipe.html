Honey Roasted Carrots

Super easy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb raw carrots
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp thyme

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 405 degrees
  2. Cut carrots in half long ways and then into 1/3 chunks across
  3. In a large bowl, mix all ingredients until carrots are evenly coated
  4. Place carrots evenly on a baking sheet and back for 35 minutes
